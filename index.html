PawHealth

Privacy, Terms and Account Deletion

PawHealth helps owners track pet care and health records. This page explains how data is handled, your rights, and how to request account deletion.

Delete Account

Request account and data deletion by email

Send your request to larymenjon.dev@gmail.com using the subject line "PawHealth Account Deletion Request".

  • Include your registered email in the message body.
  • We may ask for confirmation to protect your account security.
  • Deletion is processed within up to 15 business days after confirmation.
  • After deletion, your account profile and pet records are permanently removed.

Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 22, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following information to operate PawHealth:

  • Account details such as name, email address, and profile data.
  • Pet information, including name, species, age, weight, medication and health history records.
  • Usage information such as app interactions, crash logs, and basic device metadata.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide core app features for pet care tracking and diary/history records.
  • Improve app quality, performance and user experience.
  • Respond to support requests and account-related inquiries.
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent misuse.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be shared only with service providers required to run the app (such as hosting, analytics or crash monitoring) under confidentiality and security obligations.

4. Data Retention

We retain data while your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you request account deletion, we remove or anonymize personal data unless retention is required by law.

5. Security

We apply reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your information. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure, but we continuously improve controls.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at larymenjon.dev@gmail.com.

7. Children's Privacy

PawHealth is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child provided data, contact us to remove it.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy to reflect service or legal changes. Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
